About Learn At Home

The Learn At Home initiative, which ran from July 2020 to June 2021, brought together the Mastercard Foundation, E-Malezi and Data Science Nigeria under the Mastercard Foundation COVID-19 Recovery and Resilience Program.

The program assisted young people, in Nigeria, whose education was disrupted by the COVID-19 pandemic. The program worked with learners and educators to help them transition to online and offline digital channels for learning content delivery, student outreach and mentoring.
